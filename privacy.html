Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

MindShift Digitals ("we", "us", "our") operates the Magnetic app ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and a display name (optional).

User Content: Journal entries, worksheet responses, revision exercises, custom visualization scripts, and other content you create within the App.

Usage Data: We collect anonymized usage data such as which features you use, challenge progress, and streak information to improve the App experience.

Subscription Data: Apple handles all payment processing. We receive confirmation of your subscription status but do not have access to your payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • To sync your data across sessions and devices
  • To track your challenge progress and streaks
  • To improve the App based on usage patterns
  • To communicate important updates about the App

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Use your journal entries or personal content for marketing
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for AI training

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud platform with industry-standard encryption. Data is transmitted using HTTPS/TLS encryption.

Some data (such as challenge completions and preferences) is also cached locally on your device for offline access.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at mindshiftdigitals@gmail.com.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase (data storage and authentication)
  • Apple StoreKit (subscription management)
  • Apple AVFoundation (text-to-speech, processed on-device)

These services have their own privacy policies that govern their handling of your data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or via email. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact Us

Email: mindshiftdigitals@gmail.com
